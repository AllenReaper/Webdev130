Content

Home page

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? When someone comes to my site they will be looking for information on how to fix some sort of tech issues. Wether it be from an iphone to a TV we will have a video or an explanation on how to fix it or get it to work. What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? They will watch videos on how to use there tech that they dont know how to use or have questions about. Once they find the video that explains how to do a certain thing then the hope is that they will have learned and know how to do it. Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions? On this site we answer the nity gritty questions that your grandma or grandpa has asked you a hundred times. This site will teach from how to turn on the phone and TV to how to unsubscribe from that stupid email that keeps showing up in there inbox.
